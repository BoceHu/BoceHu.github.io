Boce Hu

I am a first year PhD student in the Khoury Collge of Computer Science at Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Robert Platt in The Helping Hands Lab. My M.S. are from Columbia University, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Sunil Agrawal and be a part of Robotics And Rehabilitation (RoAR) Lab. Before that, I earned my B.E. from Civil Aviation University of China.

I'm passionate about enabling robots to perform self-reflection and innovation, developing perception, planning, and control algorithms for robotic manipulation in unstructured environments.
